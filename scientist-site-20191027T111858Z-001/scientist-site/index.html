About me

Welcome to my website. I am a fresh PhD graduate of the Institute of Political Studies, Charles University, Prague. Prior to moving to Charles University, I studied at the Otto-von-Guericke University, at the Philipps University, and at the University of Salford.

My current research consists of multilateral institutions, rising powers, and Russian foreign policy. In terms of methods, I focus on content analysis and sentiment analysis. You can find more about my publications, teaching, and research experience on this website.
